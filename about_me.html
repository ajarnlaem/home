About Me

"Skills are cheap. Passion is priceless."

Owing to his passion for the sport, Ajarn Laem found his purpose in imparting the art of Muay Thai to all who are interested.

While he started coaching in the rural countryside of Ubon Ratchathani, he was quickly recognised for his elite coaching style and technical knowledge, and invited to coach in the city where he continued to train many amateur and professional fighters to victory.

Ajarn Laem coached at multiple world class gyms in countries such as Germany, China, Italy, and France, and worked with many prominent martial artists such as:

  • Song Yadong (UFC #7 ranked Bantamweight)
  • Mathias Gallo Cassarino (Ranked #2 Lumpinee & WPMF/WBC Champion)
  • Kem Sitsongpeenong (WMC/Thailand Champion)
  • Sitthichai Sitsongpeenong
  • Yodwicha (Lumpinee/WBC Champion)
  • Aranchai Kiatpattarapan (WBC Champion)
Ajarn Laem has a lot to offer - apart from his wealth of knowledge gathered throughout the past 30 years, his professionalism and patience is next-to-none. His constant support and encouragement for his students has made him popular and well sought after in the Muay Thai community. There will never be a dull moment in any of his classes!
