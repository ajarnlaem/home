About Me

Hailing from Ubon Ratchathani, Ajarn Laem (Maneewun Sompriao) has been teaching the art of Muay Thai for over 30 years. He went on to coach at multiple gyms around the world including Germany, China, Italy, and France, before setting his sights on Singapore. In his years of coaching at the international stage, he has worked with many prominent martial artists such as Yadong Song (UFC #15 ranked Bantamweight) and Mathias Cassarino.
