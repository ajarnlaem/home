Who am I?

Ajarn Laem is a Thai trainer from Ubon Ratchathani who has been coaching students of all levels. From absolute beginners to multiple time world champions, his notable students include Mathias Cassarino, Kem Sitsongpeenong, Song Yadong, Sitthichai Sitsongpeenong and Yodwicha.

You will learn the authentic art of Muay Thai with an emphasis on proper techniques, and building a strong foundation. Sessions are catered specifically to your current skillset and preferences. With his 30 years of pad holding experience, come feel the difference!
